# My Favorite Series: 13 Reasons Why

---

## Overview

> 13 Reasons Why is a drama series that focuses on serious real life issues faced by teenagers such as bullying depression and identity

It tells the story of a high school student named Hannah Baker who leaves behind a set of recorded tapes explaining the reasons why she decided to end her life Each tape is addressed to a specific person who played a role in her experiences

---

## Summary

The series mainly follows Clay Jensen as he listens to the tapes and discovers the truth about what happened to Hannah

> Throughout the story different characters are revealed to have contributed to her struggles in various ways

The show explores how actions words and decisions can deeply affect others It also highlights the importance of mental health awareness friendship and standing up for what is right

---

## Main Characters

| Character     | Description                    | Symbolic Role  |

| Clay Jensen   | Listener of the tapes          | conscience     |
